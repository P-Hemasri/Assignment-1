FRENCH FRIES RECEIPE
          

                                Required ingredients :

                                                    1.Potatoes 500grams
                                                    2.Oil for deep fry
                                                    3.Chilli flakes
                                                    4.Peri peri favoured masala
                                                    5.Salt for taste

                               Steps for the preparation of french fries:

                                                       1.Cut the potatoes as slices
                                                       2.Soak the potato slices 
                                                       3.Drain and dry the potatoes
                                                       4.Fry the potatoes
                                                       5.Drain and cool half done potatoes
                                                       6.Fry until golden colur to the potato slices     
                                                       7.Drain the fries
                                                       8.Add the salt and masalas to the fries
                                                       9.Serve the fries
